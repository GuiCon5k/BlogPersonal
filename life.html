Hi!

My name is Guillermo, I don't have a nickname so they just call me Guille :P, I'm from Argentina, Buenos Aires, I'm currently 21 years old, raised by beautiful parents with my two younger brothers.
I went to a normal elementary school and a secondary school with the specialization of Bachelor of Economics. So if none of my studies were about programming or anything like that, how did I get here? It was just coincidence, a friend started studying this and he told me about it so enthusiastically that I wanted to see what it was about, most of my time is always spent on the computer so I figured it wouldn't be that complicated, but when I started To see what real programming is, it was as if I had discovered a new world. Day after day programming and learning new technologies or "tricks" I liked it more and more and the hours flew by when I was programming.
I love programming but... Apart from that I have hobbies that I would love to share with you so you can get to know me a little better.

VideoGames

My oldest hobby and the ones I enjoy the most is playing video games, I literally entered this world at the age of 5 when my father bought our first computer, my first game was Pinball (In case you didn't know, there is a version for pc) since that day I never left this world and a couple of my best friends I met thanks to this world.

Gym

I was always a very athletic and sporty person, I went through many sports for example: Tae-Kwon-Do, Soccer, Swimming, Rugby and the list could go on for a long time. But there is no sport that has caught me like going to the gym, I started in 2016 and so far I can say that I already maintain it as a way of life.

Friends

One of the best things I can have in life is my friends. I love going out to eat, walking or just getting together to laugh non-stop for hours, it's a very good way for me to disconnect from the world.

My girlfriend

She is one of the best partners and friends you could have. I hang out with her as much as I can and we never get tired of it, she's just great.
